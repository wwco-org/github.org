Who we are?

We are the global organization whose mission is to bring the whole amount of knowledge to the world. Through various projects, chapters, program's, and the support structure of the non-profit WWCO organization, we strives to bring about a world in which every single human being can freely share in the sum of all knowledge.

Welcome to WWCO!

Questions

  • FKAWP (Free Knowledge Alliance of WWCO Projects) - union of free knowledge projects by the WorldWide CDI Organization, which is engaged in the implementation of projects aimed at achieving a high level of education, providing reliable information around the world.

  • The WorldWide CDI Organization is a non-profit online education organization whose main function lies in educational content and its global distribution. The organization supports the infrastructure of many scientific projects, including projects based on wiki principles: Atom, Andromeda, Terra, Mediatop, Sphere, GEL'S licenses.
